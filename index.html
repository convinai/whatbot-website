FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Whatbot.

Is this reading all my chats?

No, only the one you're viewing. Whatbot can only see the currently open chat in WhatsApp Web. It cannot access other conversations, your contact list, or any data outside the visible chat window.

Does it store anything?

Only summaries, stored locally on your device. Your messages are read, sent to AI for summarization, then immediately discarded. We don't have servers storing your chats ‚Äî we literally can't see them.

Is my data going to OpenAI or your servers?

To AI providers only, not our servers. Messages go directly to your chosen AI (Google Gemini, OpenAI, or Anthropic) for processing. We only see anonymized usage stats (request counts), never your message content.

Is this safe/legal with WhatsApp?

Yes. Whatbot is a browser extension that reads your own screen ‚Äî similar to taking a screenshot. It doesn't modify WhatsApp, access their servers, or violate their terms. You're just summarizing your own conversations.

What permissions does it need?

Three permissions: activeTab (to detect WhatsApp Web), storage (to save preferences locally), and web.whatsapp.com access (to read the visible chat). No access to other websites or browsing history.

What happens after the free trial?

After 7 days, you'll need to subscribe to continue. Your saved summaries remain accessible. If you don't subscribe, the extension simply stops generating new summaries ‚Äî no data is deleted.
